About

Sunset Tide is a Resort located in Funsville, Ontario. We specialize in a activities from fly fishing to water balloon battles. We also feature a 5-star spa which makes you feel refreshed and rejuvenated.

Activites

Activities that we have planned for the summer are water balloon battles, live rock music, yoga classes, golfing, ATV tours, wagon rides, boat rentals, canoe rental, mountain biking, and fly fishing.
